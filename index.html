About Me

Hi, Namaste visitors! I’m Mr. Ashok Sharma, proudly hailing from Chhinnamasta 03, Saptari, Madhesh Province, Nepal. As a dedicated full-time carpenter and skilled wood carving engineer, I am passionate about transforming wood into beautiful and functional works of art. My commitment to quality craftsmanship and attention to detail ensures that each piece I create is not only unique but also a testament to my love for the craft.

I specialize in crafting a variety of furniture and fixtures, including:

  • Tables: dining tables, coffee tables, and tea tables
  • Chairs: dining chairs, rocking chairs, and office chairs
  • Beds: box beds, double beds, and single beds
  • Storage Solutions: cabinets, shelves, and dressers
  • Decor Items: wooden sculptures, wall art, and custom décor
  • Small Wooden Homes: charming birdhouses, whimsical playhouses, and decorative garden sheds
  • Counters: custom counters for shops, malls, cafes, and restaurants
  • Windows and Doors: handcrafted wooden windows and doors tailored to your needs
  • Handcrafted Goddess Statues: intricately carved statues that capture the essence of divinity and culture

In addition to furniture, I can decorate a range of spaces, such as:

  • Banks
  • Offices
  • Malls
  • Restaurants
  • Cafes
  • Hotels (including lighting decorations)
  • Homes
  • Community Centers

Whether you’re looking for a stunning centerpiece for your living room, practical storage solutions, or an elegant design for your commercial space, I am here to bring your vision to life. Feel free to contact me for any custom designs you require!
